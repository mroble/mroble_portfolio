The School of Nimbus Dance Works Marketing Materials


The School of Nimbus Dance Works is a Jersey City-based dance school. I approached them initially because I wanted to help them make their web site more user-friendly. Eventually, another non-profit took over the web site revamp, so I focused on the marketing materials.


The founder, Samuel Pott, wanted to show the school's cutting edge approach to dance, while still staying true to ballet's classic style. He also wanted to introduce ballet to a community which might not be familiar with it. My solution was to create a series of mailers or postcards which would introduce different students of the school, thereby making ballet less intimidating. The brochure continued in the same vein, showing different students of the school, and clean, modern fonts. (There are two iterations of the brochure shown here. All changes were made with client feedback.) Mr. Pott opted not to use the materials eventually, but I enjoyed making the materials, and experiencing the creative process. I also created a video profile of one of their students for use at events, one of which can be seen here.
